Simple Terrain Editor by Sharp Coder

This project contains an example of a simple terrain editor (Raise/Lower, Flatten and Smooth)

- Open 'TestScene' from the Scenes folder
- Press Play then click anywhere on Terrain to sculpt it
- Pick from the available options also use sliders to tweak the area and strength
- Press W, A, S, D to move the Camera and hold right click to look around
